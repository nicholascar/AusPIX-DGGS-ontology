Description

An ontology to allow AusPIX objects to be linked into Loc-I. Two classes are defined: a AusPIX Geometry is composed of one or more AusPIX Cells. A AusPIX Geometry is a kind of geometry that may be the object of the hasGeometry property of a Loc-I Feature.
